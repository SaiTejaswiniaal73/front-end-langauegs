LORD GANESH

  • Ganesha is born on Chaturthi
  • which falls between Aug/Sep

Goddess Parvati created lord Ganesh to guard her in the absence of lord Shiva. She gave him the task of watching the home door while she bathed. In the meantime, Shiva returned home, and Ganesh, unaware of Shiva's identity, stopped him. His audacity angered Shiva, and he severed Ganesh's head

KUMAR SWAMY

  • Born on the sixth day of karthik
  • Kartikeya's symbol is the rooste

The devas asked for help against the asuras, so Parvati conceived Kartikeya. The sparks from the conception developed into six baby boys, who were raised by the Krittikas and then fused into one by Parvati.The story of Lord Kumara Swamy, also known as Lord Kartikeya, is a Hindu myth about the son of Shiva and Parvati who defeated the demon Tarakasura
